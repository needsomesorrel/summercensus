Alana Taylor

I'm Alana Taylor and I'm 15 years old. This is my second year (officially) in the Ifetayo Youth Ensemble, focusing mainly on dance and movement as my major. My project is the very website that you are on right now! It started as a place to display extra information about the census and became a place that holds all of the census work that we've done over the summer.

Kayla Touré

Kayla Touré is an second-generation West African, who is an adventurous, vivacious, and determined fifteen year old. She has starred in several productions including “Annie”, “The Little Mermaid”, “The Lion King”, “A Midsummer Night's Dream”, “Cabaret”, and others. Kayla has studied and graduated from Mark Twain For The Gifted And Talented School as a drama major. Additionally, she has had a wonderful time combining her love of acting and social justice work in her project, creating short skits to inform the public on the census and its importance. In conclusion, she thanks Ifetayo Cultural Arts Academy for this amazing opportunity.
